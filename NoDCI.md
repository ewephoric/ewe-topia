# NoDCI

NoDCI is the theory that the "SAVE THEM" murders in Five Night's at Freddy's 2, commonly interpreted as the DCI (or second Missing Children Incident), did not happen. Instead, it suggests that the event is actually a metaphor depicting the Missing Children Incident at a previous location, or that there is another explanation for the SAVE THEM bodies.

## Evidence

The "Freddy Fazbear's is Closing" newspaper at the end of FNaF 2 never mentions anything about dead children.
